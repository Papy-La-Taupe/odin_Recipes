Lasagna Recipe

A warm and traditionnal Lasagna recipe

This lasagna is packed full of flavor with layers of lasagna noodles, a rich beef and tomato sauce, a creamy white sauce and topped with cheese. Perfect for the whole family

Lasagna takes time, but it is definitely worth it. Plus it can be made in advance making it great for entertaining or a busy weekend.

Ingredients

Lasagnas are a layered meal. we won't detail each recipes here, just the finish touch, since every other part (the Bolonese & the Bechamel sauce) can be used in any other meal.

  • 1l Béchamel sauce
  • 1l Bolonese sauce
  • a few lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 160°C
  2. Put a thin Bechamel layer in the baking dish
  3. Cover it with a full layer of pasta. you can cut the corners to help them fit in
  4. Use half of the bolonese to cover it
  5. Continue with a full layer of pasta, followed by half the Bechamel
  6. Now start again : pasta, the rest of the Bolonese, then pasta, then the rest of the Bechamel.
  7. Finally, put it in the oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour
  8. Let it cool a little bit before eating
